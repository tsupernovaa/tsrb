Experience the thrill of Bangladesh railway system with our train simulator addons. Explore the beautiful routes, drive your favorite trains and have an unforgettable journey.

About Us

Train Simulator-Recreating Bangladesh is an addon for Dovetail Games's Train Simulator that lets you experience the beauty and excitement of the Bangladesh railway system. We offer a variety of routes, trains for you to enjoy. Our team of developers are passionate about creating an authentic experience that will make you feel like you're really driving a train through the stunning countryside of Bangladesh.

Routes

Explore the scenic beauty of Bangladesh with our wide range of routes. From the bustling city of Dhaka to the serene countryside of Mymensingh, we have something for everyone. Our routes are designed to provide you with a realistic experience of traveling through Bangladesh by train.

Trains

Choose from a variety of trains, each with its own unique characteristics and features. From the classic BR Class 2300 locomotives to the modern BR Class 6600 , we have a wide range of options for you to choose from. All of our trains are designed to provide you with a realistic and immersive experience.
